Experience premium care with Animal Health Management System(AHMS)

The primary objective of the- "Animal Health Management System (AHMS)" is to design and develop a comprehensive digital platform that ensures accessible, efficient, and reliable veterinary services for domestic and pet animals. The system aims to:

  • Provide routine health check-ups, vaccinations, and preventive care to improve animal well-being.
  • Facilitate diagnostic and surgical services (including ultrasound, X-ray, and dental care) with professional veterinary expertise.
  • Offer grooming and boarding facilities to ensure complete pet care and convenience for owners.
  • Enable emergency veterinary response with quick communication and reliable service availability.
  • Create a user-friendly digital platform for pet owners to register, book appointments, track medical history, and access professional guidance.
  • Enhance overall animal healthcare management through technology-driven solutions that combine compassion with professionalism.
